# Royal Hippu Family

## Overview

The current ruling dynasty is the **Hippu Dynasty**, founded by Grandfather Hippu after the fall of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty.

Only known family members are recorded here.

# Current Generation

## Hippu King

- Eldest sibling.
- Current King of 176 jungles.
- Head of the Hippu Dynasty.

---

## Second Hippu

- Younger brother of Hippu King.
- Commander under Hippu King.
- King of 16 gifted jungles.

---

## Little Brother

- Youngest sibling.
- Student at Giraffe Jungle School.
- Temporary caretaker of Rabbitu Minister's nearby Rabbitu territories.
- Subject of an ancient prophecy foretelling that he will become the greatest emperor in all three tenses of the Jungle.

# Earlier Generation

## Grandfather Hippu

Founder of the Hippu Dynasty.

Known achievements:

- Became king after the decline of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty.
- Moved the capital to Home Jungle.
- Established the Hippu royal line.
- Earned the loyalty of the two Rabbitu jungles closest to Home Jungle through principled and kind rule.

---

# Royal Rabbitu Line

The Royal Rabbitu Dynasty ruled before the Hippu Dynasty.

The known surviving royal bloodline is:

```text
Royal Rabbitu Family
        │
        │ (many generations)
        ▼

```

Most intermediate generations are unknown.

Rabbitu Minister is the last known surviving member of the Royal Rabbitu bloodline.
