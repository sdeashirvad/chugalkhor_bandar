# Territories

A territory represents a governed jungle or a collection of jungles under a ruler.

The world contains **no fixed number of jungles**.

New jungles may become known over time.

---

|-----------|---------------|-------------:|---------|-------|

# Territory Details

---

## Hippu Kingdom

**ID**

`territory_hippu_kingdom`

**Ruler**

Hippu King

**Capital**

Home Jungle

**Known Jungle Count**

176

**Government**

Direct monarchy.

Unless Hippu King appoints another official, every jungle is governed directly by him.

**Primary Institutions**

- Hippu Court
- Hippu Palace

**Known Goals**

- Maintain peace.
- Protect the kingdoms.
- Govern justly.
- Expand only when considered appropriate by Hippu King.

---

## Second Hippu Kingdom

**ID**

`territory_second_hippu_kingdom`

**Ruler**

Second Hippu

**Capital**

Border Jungle

**Known Jungle Count**

16

**Government**

Direct monarchy.

Second Hippu governs his territories personally unless he appoints someone.

**History**

Second Hippu originally possessed no jungles.

Through bravery and military victories while defending the borders, he earned Hippu King's trust.

The conquered jungles were gifted to him one by one until he became ruler of sixteen jungles.

**Military Role**

Second Hippu also serves as Commander under Hippu King.

---

## Rabbitu Kingdom

**ID**

`territory_rabbitu_kingdom`

**Current Ruler**

Rabbitu Minister

**Capital**

Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

**Confirmed Jungle Count**

2 inherited by royal gift.

Additional Rabbitu jungles may voluntarily join.

**Government**

Direct monarchy.

Temporary administration may be delegated.

Currently, Little Brother manages the two nearby Rabbitu jungles while Rabbitu Minister resides in Ancient Rabbitu Jungle.

**Primary Goal**

Reunify the independent Rabbitu chieftains under the surviving Royal Rabbitu bloodline.

Expansion is expected to occur primarily through diplomacy rather than conquest.

---

# Independent Rabbitu Jungles

Following the disappearance of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty, most Rabbitu jungles became independent under local chieftains.

Most still do not recognize Hippu rule.

Many remain undecided regarding Rabbitu Minister's claim.

Their political status may change through future stories.

---

# Historical Territories

## Ancient Royal Rabbitu Kingdom

**Former Capital**

Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

**Former Rulers**

The Royal Rabbitu Dynasty

**Status**

No longer exists as a unified kingdom.

Its former territories are now divided among independent Rabbitu chieftains, with some recognizing Rabbitu Minister and others remaining autonomous.

# Territorial Changes

Territories may change through:

- Royal gifts
- Conquest
- Diplomacy
- Succession
- Political agreements
- Abdication
- Overthrow

Ownership changes should always be recorded in the world timeline.
