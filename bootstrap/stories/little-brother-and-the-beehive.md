# Little Brother and the Beehive

## Summary

While wandering through Home Jungle, Little Brother stumbled upon a beehive hidden among the trees.

Drawn by curiosity, he slowly followed the buzzing sound.

Instead of stinging him, the bees grew calm — and the Queen Bee offered him the finest honey from the hive.

He spent the whole day listening to their stories and learning about their world.

Ever since, whenever Little Brother passes Beehive Grove, the bees buzz in melody to greet him.

# Beginning

Little Brother was exploring a quiet path through Home Jungle when he heard a steady buzzing ahead.

He moved slowly, step by step, until he found a great beehive woven among the branches.

---

# Key Events

## The Calm Bees

The hive should have been alarmed by a stranger.

Instead, the buzzing softened.

The bees did not sting Little Brother.

They seemed to recognize something gentle in him.

---

## The Queen Bee's Gift

The Queen Bee emerged and offered Little Brother the finest honey from the hive.

He accepted with wonder.

---

## A Day of Stories

Little Brother stayed the entire day.

The bees told him of their routes, their flowers, and the seasons of the Jungle as they saw them from the air.

Little Brother listened with the patience of a prince who loves to learn.

---

## The Melody That Remains

When Little Brother left at dusk, the hive did not forget him.

Now, whenever he passes Beehive Grove, the bees buzz in melody — a greeting only he receives.

Bandar smiles whenever he remembers it.

---

# Outcomes

- Little Brother formed a lasting bond with the bees of Beehive Grove.
- Beehive Grove became a place of quiet wonder in Home Jungle.
- Bandar often tells this story as an example of Little Brother's gentle curiosity.
