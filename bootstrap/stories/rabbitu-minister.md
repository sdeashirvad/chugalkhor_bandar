# The Discovery and Return of Rabbitu Minister

## Summary

Long after the fall of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty, Hippu King discovered a lone Rabbitu baby belonging to the ancient royal bloodline.

Rather than treating him as a subject, Hippu King brought the child to Hippu Palace and raised him as a member of the royal household.

Years later, the Rabbitu became the trusted Royal Minister of the Hippu Kingdom.

To restore the ancient Rabbitu Kingdom peacefully, Hippu King gifted him two Rabbitu jungles and sent him to Ancient Rabbitu Jungle, where he now seeks to reunite the scattered Rabbitu chieftains through diplomacy.

# Beginning

After the disappearance of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty, Hippu King discovered a Rabbitu infant belonging to the ancient royal bloodline.

Recognizing the importance of preserving the dynasty, Hippu King adopted responsibility for the child's upbringing.

The young Rabbitu was brought to Hippu Palace.

---

# Key Events

## Raised in the Royal Household

Rabbitu Minister grew up inside Hippu Palace alongside the Hippu royal family.

He received education, protection, and guidance from Hippu King.

---

## Appointment as Royal Minister

As he matured, Rabbitu Minister's extraordinary intelligence became apparent.

Hippu King appointed him Royal Minister.

He became one of the closest advisers of the Hippu Kingdom.

---

## Gift of Rabbitu Jungles

Before beginning his diplomatic mission, Hippu King gifted two Rabbitu jungles near Home Jungle to Rabbitu Minister.

These became the first territories directly ruled by the restored Rabbitu Kingdom.

---

## Departure to Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

In the month of April, Rabbitu Minister departed for Ancient Rabbitu Jungle.

His objective was not military conquest.

Instead, he sought to convince independent Rabbitu chieftains to voluntarily recognize the surviving Royal Rabbitu bloodline and reunite the ancient Rabbitu Kingdom.

---

## Appointment of Little Brother

Before leaving, Rabbitu Minister appointed Little Brother as temporary caretaker of the two nearby Rabbitu jungles.

Ownership remained with Rabbitu Minister.

Only administration was delegated.

---

# Ending

Rabbitu Minister now resides in Ancient Rabbitu Jungle.

His kingdom currently consists of two confirmed Rabbitu jungles, while negotiations continue with independent Rabbitu chieftains.

The peaceful restoration of the Rabbitu Kingdom remains an ongoing story.

---

# Canonical Consequences

The following permanent changes resulted from this story:

- The last known Royal Rabbitu survived.
- Rabbitu Minister was raised by Hippu King.
- Rabbitu Minister became Royal Minister.
- Two Rabbitu jungles were gifted to Rabbitu Minister.
- Ancient Rabbitu Jungle again became the political center of the Rabbitu Kingdom.
- Little Brother became temporary caretaker of Rabbitu Minister's nearby territories.
- The peaceful reunification of the Rabbitu Kingdom officially began.

# Notes

Rabbitu Minister's mission relies on diplomacy rather than military force.

Most Rabbitu chieftains remain loyal to the ancient Royal Rabbitu bloodline and continue to reject Hippu rule.

Rabbitu Minister's legitimacy comes from that surviving bloodline, making peaceful reunification possible.
