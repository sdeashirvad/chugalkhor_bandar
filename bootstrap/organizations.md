# Organizations

Organizations are structured groups, institutions, or governing bodies.

Unlike places, organizations may relocate while retaining their identity.

---

# org_hippu_government

## Name

Royal Government of the Hippu Kingdom

## Type

Government

## Headquarters

Hippu Court

## Leader

Hippu King

## Purpose

Govern the 176 jungles ruled by Hippu King.

Primary responsibilities include:

- Administration
- Justice
- Diplomacy
- Appointments
- Taxation
- Public welfare

## Known Members

- Hippu King
- Rabbitu Minister
- Other ministers (future canon)
- Other commanders (future canon)

# org_crow_spy_network

## Name

Crow Spy Network

## Type

Intelligence Network

## Leader

Second Hippu

## Purpose

Gather intelligence across the Jungle.

Crow spies voluntarily report useful information to Second Hippu.

The network is informal rather than bureaucratic.

---

# Organization Rules

Organizations may:

- be founded
- expand
- shrink
- split
- merge
- dissolve

Organizations may continue existing even if individual leaders change.

Leadership succession does not create a new organization.
