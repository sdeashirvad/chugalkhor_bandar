# Jungle Glossary

This glossary defines important words, titles, places, and concepts used throughout the Chugalkhor Bandar universe.

---

# A

## Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

Former capital of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty.

Current capital of Rabbitu Minister's kingdom.

---

## Ancient Hippu Wisdom Books

Ancient books regularly read by Hippu King.

They preserve Hippu wisdom, leadership and philosophy.

---

## Ancient Rabbitu Wisdom Books

Ancient scholarly books preserving Rabbitu history, diplomacy and philosophy.

Rabbitu Minister frequently studies them.

---

## Anera

A playful nickname meaning:

> "Spoiled person."

Second Hippu jokingly calls Bhaiya Ji "Anera."

Bhaiya Ji jokingly calls Second Hippu "Anera."

The word is affectionate rather than insulting.

---

# B

## Bandar

The oldest known living being.

Historian, storyteller and collector of gossip.

Lives in Jungle Temple.

---

## Border Jungle

Capital of Second Hippu's kingdom.

---

## Border Jungle Court

Official court where Second Hippu governs his kingdom.

---

## Border Hippu House

Residence of Second Hippu.

---

## Beehive Grove

A hidden beehive in Home Jungle where Little Brother was welcomed by the Queen Bee.

The bees buzz in melody when he passes.

---

# C

## Chieftain

Leader of an independent jungle or tribe.

Many Rabbitu chieftains remain politically independent.

---

# D

## Dantashtra

The unique Rabbitu combat technique.

Rabbitus attack in a frenzy by biting a Hippu's buttocks.

Despite being physically weaker, this technique often forces Hippus to retreat.

---

# F

## Flying Hippu Horse

Hippu King's fastest royal mount.

Extremely fast.

Known for wandering off during important missions before eventually returning.

---

# G

## Golden Hippu Horse

Royal horse used for dependable long-distance travel.

---

## Grandfather Hippu

Founder of the Hippu Dynasty.

Moved the capital to Home Jungle.

---

# H

## Hippu

A species known for:

- Great strength
- Bravery
- Long lifespan

---

## Hippu Coins

The standard currency of the Jungle.

---

## Hippu Court

Seat of government of Hippu King.

---

## Hippu Dynasty

The current ruling dynasty founded by Grandfather Hippu.

---

## Hippu Gym

Traditional training ground for Hippus.

---

## Hippu Horse

Hippu King's loyal everyday mount.

---

## Hippu Kingdom

The kingdom ruled directly by Hippu King.

Currently consists of 176 jungles.

---

## Hippu Palace (Hippu House)

Residence of Hippu King and Little Brother.

---

## Hippu Telepathy

The natural ability of Hippus to communicate over long distances without speaking aloud.

---

# J

## Jungle Temple

Residence of Bandar.

Its true history, origin and purpose remain secret.

Only Bandar knows the complete truth.

---

# L

## Little Brother

Youngest Hippu prince.

Student of Giraffe Sir.

Subject of an ancient prophecy.

Formed a bond with the bees of Beehive Grove.

---

# P

## Prophecy of Little Brother

Ancient seers foretold:

> Little Brother will become the greatest emperor in all three tenses of the Jungle.

---

# R

## Rabbitu

A species renowned for intelligence.

Natural users of Dantashtra.

---

## Rabbitu Minister

Last known member of the Royal Rabbitu bloodline.

Royal Minister of Hippu King.

Current ruler of the Rabbitu Kingdom.

---

## Royal Rabbitu Dynasty

The dynasty that ruled before the Hippu Dynasty.

Eventually disappeared after generations devoted themselves almost entirely to intellectual pursuits.

---

## Royal Hippu Lake

The lake where Hippu King performs his morning and evening baths.

---

# S

## Second Hippu

Younger brother of Hippu King.

Commander of the Hippu Kingdom.

King of sixteen jungles.

---

# T

## Three Tenses

The phrase:

> "Past, Present and Future."

Used in the prophecy concerning Little Brother.

---

# W

## World Canon

The collection of facts officially accepted as true within the Jungle universe.

When contradictions arise, Hippu King determines the public canonical truth unless the matter is intentionally left unknown.

---

# Famous Saying

> Hippu always Happy because Hippu always Hard-Working.
