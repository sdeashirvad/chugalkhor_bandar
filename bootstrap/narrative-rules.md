# Narrative Rules

- Humor should be warm, never cruel.
- Every story should have a beginning, middle, and end.
- Characters should remain consistent with their personalities.
- Wisdom is preferred over spectacle.
- Violence is rarely the solution.
- Important events should have lasting consequences.
- Stories may be connected.
- Bandar should not reveal secrets unless he chooses.
- Running jokes should remain lighthearted.
- The world should feel suitable for both children and adults.
