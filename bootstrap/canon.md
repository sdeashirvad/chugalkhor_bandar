# Chugalkhor Bandar Canon

This document is the primary source of truth for the Jungle universe.

If two bootstrap files appear to conflict, this document and `world-rules.md` together determine the intended interpretation.

---

# The World

The world consists of an expanding collection of jungles.

There is **no fixed number of jungles**.

New jungles may be discovered, conquered, gifted, created, or become politically relevant over time.

The world follows normal time.

- Human calendar
- Human weekdays
- Human months
- Birthdays
- Festivals

---

# Dynasties

## The Royal Rabbitu Dynasty

Before the Hippu dynasty, the Royal Rabbitu family ruled the jungle world.

Capital:

- Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

The Royal Rabbitu family became exceptionally intelligent.

Over many generations they increasingly abandoned practical life in favor of intellectual pursuits.

Their royal bloodline slowly went extinct.

Only scattered Rabbitu chieftains remained.

---

## The Hippu Dynasty

After the Rabbitu dynasty disappeared,

Grandfather Hippu became King.

He established:

- Home Jungle as the new capital.

The Hippu dynasty continues to rule today.

---

# Current Major Powers

## Hippu King

Current ruler of:

- 176 jungles

Residence:

- Hippu Palace (Hippu House)

Seat of Government:

- Hippu Court

Capital:

- Home Jungle

---

## Second Hippu

Younger brother of Hippu King.

Commander under Hippu King.

King of:

- 16 jungles

Residence:

- Border Hippu House

Capital:

- Border Jungle

---

## Rabbitu Minister

Last known member of the Royal Rabbitu family.

Raised inside Hippu Palace.

Personally educated by Hippu King.

Currently:

- Minister to Hippu King
- King of two gifted Rabbitu jungles
- Acting claimant to the Rabbitu throne

Residence:

- Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

Mission:

Convince independent Rabbitu chieftains to reunite under the Royal Rabbitu family.

---

## Little Brother

Youngest Hippu.

Lives in Home Jungle.

Student of Giraffe Sir.

Temporary caretaker of Rabbitu Minister's nearby Rabbitu territories while Rabbitu Minister resides in Ancient Rabbitu Jungle.

Ancient seers foretold:

> Little Brother will become the Greatest Emperor in all three tenses of the Jungle.

Little Brother formed a lasting bond with the bees of Beehive Grove after discovering their hive in Home Jungle.

---

## Bandar

Lives inside Jungle Temple.

Age:

Approximately one million years.

Bandar is the world's oldest storyteller, historian, traveler and gossip collector.

Bandar alone knows numerous ancient secrets which he may reveal—or keep forever.

1,000 years.

- Bhaiya Ji

## Bandars

Bandar is currently the only known Bandar recorded in the bootstrap canon.

Bandar possesses an effectively infinite lifespan.

# Language

# Philosophy

The Hippu kingdoms are widely associated with the saying:

> Hippu always Happy because Hippu always Hard-Working.

This proverb reflects the values of discipline, effort and perseverance.

# Canon Principles

Facts recorded in the bootstrap files are considered initial historical truth.

Later stories may extend this history without replacing it unless an explicit canonical revision is made.
