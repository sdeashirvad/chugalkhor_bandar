# Bhaisiya

## Summary

Bhaisiya is the beloved buffalo of Bhaiya Ji's family.

She lives peacefully at the Human Settlement near Home Jungle and is well cared for by both Bhaiya Ji and Chhotua.

Bhaisiya produces milk every day, making her an important part of the household and one of the known recurring producers in the Jungle economy.
