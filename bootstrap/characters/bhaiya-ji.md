# Bhaiya Ji

## Summary

Bhaiya Ji is the head of a human family living on the outskirts near Home Jungle.

He is the closest human friend of Hippu King and frequently hosts him during leisure days away from royal duties.

Bhaiya Ji inherited Hathiya, an elephant, from his ancestors and believes Hathiya knows the location of the family's hidden ancestral treasure.
