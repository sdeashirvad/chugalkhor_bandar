# Second Hippu

## Summary

Second Hippu is the younger brother of Hippu King and serves as the Commander of the Hippu Kingdom.

Unlike his elder brother, he inherited no jungles. Through exceptional bravery, military victories, and unwavering loyalty, he earned Hippu King's trust and was gradually gifted sixteen jungles, becoming their ruler.

Although one of the strongest Hippus alive, he is also humorous, carefree, and occasionally lazy when peace prevails.

# History

Second Hippu began with no kingdom of his own.

Stationed at Border Jungle, he repeatedly defended Hippu territory and won new jungles through military campaigns.

Each success earned Hippu King's appreciation, who rewarded him with additional jungles.

Over many years, these royal gifts grew into his present kingdom of sixteen jungles.

As Hippu King's priorities shifted from conquest to peaceful governance, Second Hippu's military responsibilities became much lighter.

A typical day consists of:

6. Frequently communicate using Hippu Telepathy.
