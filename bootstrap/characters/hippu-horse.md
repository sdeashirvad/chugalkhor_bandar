# Hippu Horse

## Summary

The Hippu Horse is Hippu King's everyday royal mount.

It lives at Hippu Palace and is used primarily for short-distance travel around Home Jungle and nearby territories.

Although it is the slowest of the three royal horses, it is also the most loyal and dependable companion of Hippu King.

# Personality

The Hippu Horse is:

- Extremely loyal
- Calm
- Gentle
- Patient
- Dependable
- Hard-working

Unlike the Flying Hippu Horse, it never wanders away from duty.

Unlike the Golden Hippu Horse, it prefers staying close to Hippu Palace rather than traveling great distances.

It finds satisfaction in faithfully serving Hippu King every day.

---

# History

The Hippu Horse has faithfully served Hippu King for many years.

Whenever Hippu King needs to travel around Home Jungle or nearby locations, the Hippu Horse is almost always his first choice.

Its reliability has earned complete trust from the royal family.

---

# Daily Life

A typical day consists of:

1. Living at Hippu Palace.
2. Resting in the royal stables.
3. Carrying Hippu King to nearby destinations.
4. Returning to Hippu Palace.
5. Spending the remainder of the day close to the royal household.
