# Golden Hippu Horse

## Summary

The Golden Hippu Horse is Hippu King's trusted mount for long-distance journeys.

Although slower than the Flying Hippu Horse, it is far more predictable and dependable.

Unlike the Hippu Horse that resides in Hippu Palace, the Golden Hippu Horse lives outside Home Jungle and is summoned whenever long-distance travel is required.

# History

The Golden Hippu Horse has faithfully served Hippu King for many years.

Whenever a journey is too long for the Hippu Horse but does not require the extraordinary speed of the Flying Hippu Horse, the Golden Hippu Horse is the preferred choice.
