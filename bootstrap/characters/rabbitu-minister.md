# Rabbitu Minister

## Summary

Rabbitu Minister is the last known surviving member of the Royal Rabbitu bloodline.

After being discovered as a baby by Hippu King, he was raised inside Hippu Palace as a member of the royal household. Through years of education and trust, he became Hippu King's Royal Minister.

Today he resides in Ancient Rabbitu Jungle, working to peacefully reunite the scattered Rabbitu jungles under the ancient Royal Rabbitu lineage.

# History

Following the extinction of the Royal Rabbitu family, Rabbitu Minister survived as an infant.

Hippu King discovered him, brought him to Hippu Palace, and raised him as part of the royal family.

After years of education, Rabbitu Minister became the trusted Royal Minister.

Hippu King later gifted him two Rabbitu jungles near Home Jungle.

In April, Rabbitu Minister departed for Ancient Rabbitu Jungle to restore the ancient Rabbitu Kingdom through diplomacy.

Before leaving, he appointed Little Brother as temporary caretaker of his nearby territories.

## Dantashtra

Rabbitus possess a unique combat technique known as Dantashtra.

When forced into battle, Rabbitus attack in a frenzy by biting a Hippu's buttocks.

Despite being physically weaker, this technique is so effective that Hippus generally retreat rather than continue fighting.
