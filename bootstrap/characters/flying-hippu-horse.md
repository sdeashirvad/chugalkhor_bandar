# Flying Hippu Horse

## Summary

The Flying Hippu Horse is Hippu King's fastest royal mount.

Capable of traveling from Home Jungle to Border Jungle in approximately one day, it is reserved for urgent missions and long-distance journeys.

Despite its unmatched speed, it is famous for its mischievous personality. Every so often, instead of completing an assigned mission immediately, it wanders off on adventures of its own before eventually returning.
