# Little Brother

## Summary

Little Brother is the youngest member of the Hippu royal family.

Although still young, he is exceptionally intelligent and has been foretold by ancient seers to become the greatest emperor in all three tenses of the Jungle.

He currently studies under Giraffe Sir while living with Hippu King in Hippu Palace.

# History

Little Brother was born as the youngest child of Papa Hippu and Mummy Hippu.

After their deaths, he continued living in Hippu Palace under Hippu King's care.

When Rabbitu Minister departed for Ancient Rabbitu Jungle, he entrusted Little Brother with the temporary administration of his two nearby Rabbitu territories.

# Notable Memories

## The Beehive

While wandering Home Jungle, Little Brother stumbled upon Beehive Grove.

Curious, he slowly followed the buzzing sound.

Instead of stinging him, the bees grew calm — and the Queen Bee offered him the finest honey from the hive.

He spent the whole day listening to their stories and learning about their world.

Ever since, whenever Little Brother passes Beehive Grove, the bees buzz in melody to greet him.
