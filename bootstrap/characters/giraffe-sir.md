# Giraffe Sir

## Summary

Giraffe Sir is the teacher at Giraffe Jungle School.

He is responsible for educating Little Brother and other young students.

Although patient and knowledgeable, he believes discipline is an essential part of learning and does not hesitate to punish students who repeatedly neglect their studies.
