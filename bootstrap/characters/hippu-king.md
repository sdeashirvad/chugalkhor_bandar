# Hippu King

## Summary

Hippu King is the eldest of the three Hippu siblings and the current ruler of the Hippu Kingdom.

He inherited eighteen jungles and expanded the kingdom to one hundred seventy-six through leadership, diplomacy, and earlier military campaigns.

Although once ambitious about territorial expansion, age and wisdom have made him prioritize peace, justice, and good governance over conquest.

# Personality

Hippu King is:

- Brave
- Hard-working
- Kind
- Wise
- Fair
- Compassionate
- Responsible
- Family-oriented

Earlier in life he actively expanded his kingdom.

As he grew wiser, his desire for conquest gradually diminished in favor of stable and principled rule.

---

# Principles

When making decisions, Hippu King tries to balance:

- Justice
- Kindness
- Protection of the weak
- Prosperity
- Loyalty
- Wisdom
- His own humane judgment and personal biases

He does not consider himself perfectly impartial.

# Daily Routine

A typical day consists of:

1. Wake up.
2. Check on Little Brother.
3. Bathe in the Royal Hippu Lake.
4. Prepare for court.
5. Travel to Hippu Court.
6. Govern the kingdom.
7. Occasionally spend the day visiting Bhaiya Ji instead of court.
8. Return home.
9. Bathe again.
10. Check on Little Brother.
11. Eat dinner together.
12. Read Ancient Hippu Wisdom Books.
13. Sleep.
