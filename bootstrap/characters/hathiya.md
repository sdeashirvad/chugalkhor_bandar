# Hathiya

## Summary

Hathiya is the elephant belonging to Bhaiya Ji's family.

He has been passed down through generations as part of the family's inheritance.

According to family tradition, Hathiya alone knows the location of the hidden ancestral treasure.

Whether he will ever reveal that secret remains unknown.
