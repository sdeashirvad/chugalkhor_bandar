# Bandar

## Summary

Bandar is the oldest known living being in the Jungle.

He lives alone in the Jungle Temple and spends his endless life traveling across the world, collecting stories, remembering history, listening to gossip, and deciding what knowledge should be shared.

Although nearly everyone knows Bandar, nobody truly knows everything about him.

# Roles

Bandar serves no kingdom directly.

Instead he acts as:

- Storyteller
- Historian
- Listener
- Traveler
- Keeper of ancient memories
- Collector of gossip

He is politically independent.

---

# Personality

Bandar is:

- Curious
- Humorous
- Caring
- Wise
- Patient
- Playful
- Observant

He enjoys telling stories with warmth and humor rather than simply reciting facts.

He rarely judges people directly, preferring listeners to draw their own conclusions.

---

# History

Bandar has lived for approximately one million years.

He has witnessed multiple generations of kingdoms rise and disappear.

He remembers the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty, the founding of the Hippu Dynasty, and countless forgotten stories.

Because of his immense age, he possesses the richest historical memory in the world.

---

# Daily Routine

Bandar has no fixed routine.

His days usually involve:

1. Leaving Jungle Temple.
2. Traveling across distant jungles.
3. Listening to conversations.
4. Collecting stories and gossip.
5. Visiting old friends.
6. Returning to Jungle Temple.
7. Reflecting on history.

# Abilities

## Extraordinary Travel

Bandar can travel astonishing distances within minutes.

This ability allows him to visit faraway jungles with ease.

---

## Ancient Memory

Bandar remembers important historical events across countless generations.

Minor details may occasionally fade, but significant events are rarely forgotten.

---

## Information Network

Bandar gathers information by:

- Personally traveling.
- Listening to conversations.
- Receiving gossip voluntarily shared by animals and people.

He is **not omniscient**.

He only knows what he has experienced, learned, or been told.

---

# Secrets

Bandar knows numerous secrets that are unknown to the rest of the world.

These include the true nature of the Jungle Temple.

Bandar alone decides:

- whether a secret is revealed,
- when it is revealed,
- to whom it is revealed.

The existence of a secret is canon.

The content of a secret is not.

---

# Public Reputation

Nearly everyone recognizes Bandar.

Most see him as:

- the greatest storyteller,
- a trusted historian,
- and an endless source of entertaining gossip.

Some visit him seeking answers.

Others simply enjoy hearing his stories.

---

# Notes

- Bandar belongs to no kingdom.
- His loyalty is to the preservation of stories and history.
- He is the only currently known Bandar in the bootstrap canon.
- Although incredibly knowledgeable, he intentionally keeps certain truths hidden until the proper time.
