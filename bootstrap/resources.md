# Resources

Resources are consumable or tradeable commodities used throughout the Jungle.

Unlike objects, resources are generally interchangeable and are produced continuously.

---

# Currency

## res_hippu_coins

**Name**

Hippu Coins

**Category**

Currency

**Description**

The official currency of the known Jungle kingdoms.

Used for:

- Taxes
- Trade
- Gifts
- Salaries
- Rewards

**Produced By**

Kings through taxation and circulation.

# Resource Lifecycle

Resources may be:

- Produced
- Harvested
- Collected
- Consumed
- Gifted
- Traded
- Purchased
- Sold
- Taxed
- Stored
- Spoiled (if a future story establishes this)

---

# Taxation

Taxes are collected by the ruler of each jungle.

Current rulers include:

- Hippu King
- Second Hippu
- Rabbitu Minister

The ruler decides:

- when taxes are collected,
- how often they are collected,
- and how they are distributed.

Taxes are usually collected in Hippu Coins, although stories may establish taxation in other resources when appropriate.

---

# Production

Known recurring production includes:

| Producer | Resource | Frequency |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Bhaisiya | Milk | Daily |

Additional producers may be introduced through future stories.

---

# Trade

Resources may freely move between individuals and kingdoms through:

- Trade
- Gifts
- Taxation
- Rewards
- Commerce
- Aid

Ownership of resources is temporary and expected to change frequently.
