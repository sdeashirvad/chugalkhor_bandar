# Places

This document lists all known named places in the Jungle universe.

Political ownership belongs in `territories.md`.

This file describes the physical places themselves.

---

# Home Jungle

## ID

`place_home_jungle`

## Type

Capital Jungle

## Description

Home Jungle is the capital of the Hippu Dynasty.

It serves as the political and cultural center of Hippu civilization.

Hippu King and Little Brother both live here.

Many important royal institutions are located within Home Jungle.

## Current Ruler

Hippu King

## Important Locations

- Hippu Palace
- Hippu Court
- Royal Hippu Lake

## Connected Places

- Border Jungle
- Ancient Rabbitu Jungle
- Human Settlement

---

# Border Jungle

## ID

`place_border_jungle`

## Type

Capital Jungle

## Description

Border Jungle is the capital of Second Hippu's kingdom.

It was the base from which Second Hippu defended and expanded the Hippu kingdoms.

Although warfare has become much rarer, it remains an important military territory.

## Current Ruler

Second Hippu

## Important Locations

- Border Hippu House
- Border Jungle Court
- Hippu Gym

## Connected Places

- Home Jungle

---

# Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

## ID

`place_ancient_rabbitu_jungle`

## Type

Ancient Capital

## Description

Ancient Rabbitu Jungle was once the capital of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty.

Today it serves as Rabbitu Minister's residence and headquarters for the peaceful reunification of the Rabbitu kingdoms.

It remains culturally important to Rabbitus.

## Current Ruler

Rabbitu Minister

## Connected Places

- Home Jungle

# Beehive Grove

## ID

`place_beehive_grove`

## Type

Natural Landmark

## Description

A hidden beehive woven among the trees of Home Jungle.

Little Brother discovered it while wandering the jungle paths.

The bees here are unusually calm toward him.

When Little Brother passes, they buzz in melody to greet him.

## Located In

Home Jungle

## Notable Visitors

- Little Brother
- Bandar

---

# Hippu Court

## ID

`place_hippu_court`

## Type

Royal Court

## Description

Seat of government of the Hippu Kingdom.

Hippu King conducts official state business here.

## Functions

- Royal audiences
- Petitions
- Diplomatic meetings
- Appointments
- Territorial decisions
- Gifts of jungles
- Public announcements

## Regular Visitors

- Hippu King
- Ministers
- Commanders
- Citizens
- Foreign rulers
- Other visitors unless restricted

## Located In

Home Jungle

# Jungle Temple

## ID

`place_jungle_temple`

## Type

Temple

## Description

Residence of Bandar.

One of the oldest known places in the Jungle.

The true origin, history, purpose, and powers of the temple remain unknown.

These secrets are known only to Bandar.

## Current Resident

Bandar

# Human Settlement

## ID

`place_human_settlement`

## Type

Village

## Description
