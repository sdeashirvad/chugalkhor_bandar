# World Timeline

This document records the chronological sequence of major historical events in the Jungle universe.

Individual story files contain the detailed narrative.

---

# Ancient Era

## ~1,000,000+ years ago

### Bandar begins his endless journey

Bandar is already alive during this period.

Over the ages he becomes:

- Historian
- Storyteller
- Gossip collector
- Keeper of memories

He witnesses countless generations of Jungle history.

---

# Rabbitu Era

## The Royal Rabbitu Dynasty

The Royal Rabbitu family rules the Jungle.

Capital:

- Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

Rabbitus become renowned for intelligence, scholarship and philosophy.

---

## Decline of the Royal Rabbitu Dynasty

Over many generations, the Royal Rabbitu family increasingly devotes itself to intellectual pursuits.

The royal bloodline gradually disappears.

Independent Rabbitu chieftains emerge throughout the Jungle.

---

# Founding Era

## Rise of Grandfather Hippu

Grandfather Hippu becomes king.

The Hippu Dynasty is founded.

---

## Capital Relocated

The capital moves from:

Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

to

Home Jungle.

---

## Recognition by Nearby Rabbitu Jungles

The two Rabbitu jungles nearest Home Jungle voluntarily recognize Grandfather Hippu as king after experiencing his principled leadership.

Most Rabbitu jungles remain independent.

---

# Early Hippu Era

## Birth of the Three Hippu Siblings

Born to:

- Papa Hippu
- Mummy Hippu

Children:

- Hippu King
- Second Hippu
- Little Brother

---

## Death of Papa Hippu and Mummy Hippu

Before their deaths, they establish Hippu King as ruler.

---

## Hippu King inherits 18 jungles

Hippu King begins his reign.

---

## Discovery of Rabbitu Minister

Hippu King discovers a surviving Rabbitu infant of the ancient Royal Rabbitu bloodline.

The child is brought to Hippu Palace.

---

## Rabbitu Minister is Raised

Rabbitu Minister grows up within the Hippu royal household.

---

# Expansion Era

## Second Hippu becomes Commander

Second Hippu begins defending Border Jungle.

---

## Expansion of the Hippu Kingdom

Military victories gradually expand Hippu King's kingdom.

---

## Sixteen Jungles Gifted

Second Hippu earns sixteen jungles through bravery and loyalty.

Border Jungle becomes the capital of his kingdom.

---

## Hippu Kingdom expands to 176 jungles

The Hippu Kingdom reaches its currently known size.

---

# Modern Era

## Rabbitu Minister becomes Royal Minister

Rabbitu Minister becomes Hippu King's chief minister.

---

## Gift of Two Rabbitu Jungles

Hippu King gifts two Rabbitu jungles near Home Jungle to Rabbitu Minister.

---

## Rabbitu Minister departs for Ancient Rabbitu Jungle

**Month**

April

Mission:

Peacefully reunite the Rabbitu Kingdom.

---

## Little Brother becomes Temporary Caretaker

Rabbitu Minister appoints Little Brother as caretaker of the two nearby Rabbitu jungles during his absence.

---

## Present Day

Current rulers:

### Hippu King

- Rules 176 jungles.

---

### Second Hippu

- Rules 16 jungles.
- Commander of the Hippu Kingdom.

---

### Rabbitu Minister

- Rules two confirmed Rabbitu jungles.
- Pursues peaceful reunification.

---

### Little Brother

- Studies under Giraffe Sir.
- Lives in Hippu Palace.
- Subject of the ancient prophecy.
- Formed a bond with the bees of Beehive Grove; they buzz in melody when he passes.

---

### Bandar

Continues traveling the Jungle, collecting stories and preserving history.

---

# Future

Known unresolved developments:

- Rabbitu Kingdom reunification.
- Expansion of Rabbitu Minister's influence.
- Fulfillment of Little Brother's prophecy.
- Discovery of the Hidden Treasure known only to Hathiya.

# Timeline Maintenance Rules

Whenever a story permanently changes the world:

1. Update the relevant story file.
2. Add the event to this timeline.
3. Record any lasting political, territorial or relationship changes.
4. Preserve historical order without rewriting earlier events.
